Questions

Frequently Asked

What file formats do you accept?
We accept WAV, AIFF, FLAC, and MP3. For mixing, please export your stems as WAV or AIFF at 24-bit, 44.1kHz or higher. Zip your stems folder before uploading.
How many revisions do I get?
Starter packages include 2 revisions. Premium and Bundle packages include unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied with the result.
How long does it take?
Standard turnaround is 3-7 business days depending on the package. Rush delivery (24-48 hours) is available as an add-on for urgent projects.
Do I retain full ownership of my music?
Absolutely. You retain 100% ownership of your music. We never claim any rights to your work.
Can I hear samples before ordering?
Yes! Check out our Before & After demos section to hear the DGUNZ difference across different genres.

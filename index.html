Our Process

A simple, transparent process from order to delivery.

📝
Step 1

Choose Your Package & Place Your Order

Browse our three resume writing packages — Basic, Professional, and Executive. Select the one that matches your career level, fill in the order form with your details, and complete your secure payment.

Takes less than 5 minutes. You'll receive an instant confirmation email.

🎯
Step 2

Fill Out Your Career Questionnaire

After ordering, you'll receive a short questionnaire asking about your career goals, target role, key achievements, and work history. The more detail you provide, the stronger your resume will be.

You can also upload your existing resume as a starting point.

✍️
Step 3

Expert Writer Crafts Your Resume

A certified career writer with expertise in your industry is assigned to your order. They write a custom, ATS-optimized resume tailored to your target role — not a template.

Your resume is delivered within 24–48 hours depending on your package.

🚀
Step 4

Review, Revise & Download

You'll receive your resume by email. Review it and request any changes — we offer free revisions with every package. Once you're happy, download your final resume in Word and PDF formats.

You're ready to apply and land your dream job!

What's Included

Full Mixing Service

Mixing is the process where individual elements of a song are balanced and shaped into a cohesive, powerful record. DGUNZ approaches every mix to translate perfectly across all listening environments.

  • EQ and frequency shaping per track
  • Compression and dynamic control
  • Pitch correction (Auto-Tune where appropriate)
  • De-essing for vocal clarity
  • Multi-band compression
  • Stereo panning and spatial imaging
  • Level balancing across all stems
  • Automation — volume, effects, transitions
  • Reverb, delay, and creative effects design

Mastering

What's Included

Full Mastering Service

Mastering is the final stage that prepares your music for professional release. DGUNZ applies Apple Digital Masters certified techniques to ensure every master sounds incredible across every platform.

  • Dynamic EQ adjustments and tonal balancing
  • Multi-band compression for controlled dynamics
  • Harmonic saturation for warmth and character
  • Stereo widening via Mid-Side processing
  • Loudness optimization (LUFS targeting per platform)
  • True Peak limiting
  • Streaming platform compatibility
  • Apple Digital Masters standard compliance
  • Dithering for bit-depth reduction
Stereo vs Stem Mastering
Stereo Mastering
Full stereo mix processed and optimized. Ideal for most releases.
Stem Mastering
Individual stems processed separately for maximum control. +$99 add-on.
Spatial / Immersive
Next-gen immersive audio mastering for Dolby Atmos compatible platforms.

The Story

DGUNZ is a professional mixing and mastering engineer dedicated to helping artists turn great recordings into powerful, release-ready music. As the Founder and Lead Engineer of DGUNZ Audio, he personally oversees every project to ensure the highest level of sonic quality and consistency.

With a Distinction in Mixing & Mastering from Point Blank Music School (London) and certification in Apple Digital Masters, DGUNZ combines formal training with over a decade of hands-on experience shaping records across multiple genres.

Earlier in his career, DGUNZ was managed by Universal Music Group (UMG) Nigeria. Since beginning his engineering journey in 2007, he has worked with artists, producers, and content creators around the world. His engineering work has reached over 2 million streams worldwide.

Why You’re in the Right Place

Professional mixing and mastering for artists who want their music to sound world-class everywhere it is heard.

For over 15 years, our engineers have worked with thousands of artists across genres and around the world, including several A-list artists. What's Included

Full Mixing Service

Every mix at DGUNZ Audio is handled with the same level of care and attention that major-label records receive. Nothing is template-driven — each track is approached fresh, with a custom engineering strategy built around your sound, your genre, and the emotion you're trying to convey.

  • Full stem-by-stem processing — every element gets individual treatment
  • Precision EQ to remove harshness, add clarity, and let each element breathe
  • Dynamic compression that controls energy without killing the life of your track
  • Vocal production — tuning, de-essing, saturation, and clarity shaping
  • Creative effects design — reverbs, delays, and spatial movement that serve the song
  • Stereo imaging and depth placement for a wide, immersive soundstage
  • Detailed automation — every transition, drop, and build refined to hit harder
  • Low-end management for powerful, translation-ready bass across speakers and headphones
  • Streaming-optimized final bounce — loud, clear, and competitive on any platform

What's Included

Full Mastering Service

Mastering is the final stage that prepares your music for professional release. DGUNZ applies Apple Digital Masters certified techniques to ensure every master sounds incredible across every platform.

  • Dynamic EQ adjustments and tonal balancing
  • Multi-band compression for controlled dynamics
  • Harmonic saturation for warmth and character
  • Stereo widening via Mid-Side processing
  • Loudness optimization (LUFS targeting per platform)
  • True Peak limiting
  • Streaming platform compatibility
  • Apple Digital Masters standard compliance
  • Dithering for bit-depth reduction
Stereo vs Stem Mastering
Stereo Mastering
Full stereo mix processed and optimized. Ideal for most releases.
Stem Mastering
Individual stems processed separately for maximum control. +$99 add-on.
Spatial / Immersive
Next-gen immersive audio mastering for Dolby Atmos compatible platforms.

    Refund Policy

    Fair, transparent terms designed to protect both our clients and our work.

    At mixingandmasteringengineers.com, your satisfaction matters to us. Because our services are digital and non-retrievable, this Refund Policy is designed to be fair, transparent, and client-focused while protecting the integrity of our work.

    Our Promise to You

    We do not simply deliver a track and move on. We work with you to understand your vision, apply your feedback carefully, and move the record toward a release-ready standard.

    Our goal is always to give every project a final result you feel confident using.

    Revisions Come First

    If you are not fully satisfied with your track, revisions are the first step. We offer reasonable revisions so your feedback can be reviewed and applied properly before any refund request is considered.

    Most concerns are resolved during this stage, and we take that process seriously.

    When Refunds May Apply

    A refund may be considered only after multiple reasonable revision attempts have been made and we have made genuine efforts to meet your expectations, but you remain truly unsatisfied with the final result.

    Approved refunds may be full or partial, depending on the circumstances of the project and the work already completed.

    Important Fair Use Policy

    If a delivered track is used, released, distributed, uploaded, monetized, or clearly intended for use, a refund will not be granted.

    This policy exists because our work is delivered digitally and cannot be returned once it has been sent to the client. We have had situations where a client received a version they liked, kept the work, and still requested a refund. To keep the process fair for everyone, retained or used work is not eligible for refund.

    Customer Responsibility

    • You understand that mixing and mastering are creative and somewhat subjective services.
    • You agree that revisions are the primary method for improving results.
    • You confirm that refund decisions are handled case by case and are not automatic.
    • You agree to use our services in good faith.

    Contact

    If you have concerns before or after placing an order, please contact us before assuming the issue cannot be resolved. We are always willing to review genuine cases fairly.
